MOSS GALLERIES' STORY


Antiquarian books are those that hold value due to their edition, the year of printing, quality of printing, binding, illustrations or provenance. This curated collection features books published between the 18th and 20th centuries, focused on a large and diverse array of topics. This collection of books is a rich chronicle from the past and of the past, filled with excellent lithographs, maps and photographs that allow the reader to paint a picture of life in the centuries gone by.
